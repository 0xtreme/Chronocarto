About Chronocarto

A spatiotemporal atlas of human conflict. Scrub through 5,000 years of history and watch wars bloom and recede across geography.

Data Sources

This dataset is curated from established scholarly and historical sources including:

  • UCDP/PRIO — Uppsala Conflict Data Program (modern conflicts, 1946–present)
  • Correlates of War — Interstate and civil wars (1816–present)
  • Brecke Conflict Catalog — Historical conflicts (1400–present)
  • Seshat Databank — Deep history conflicts
  • Wikipedia / scholarly literature — Individual battle and war records

Casualty figures are shown as ranges reflecting scholarly uncertainty. Dates marked "approximate" or "disputed" indicate limited historical precision.

Controls

  • Timeline — Drag the thumbs or the highlighted range
  • Play — Animates a 100-year window through time (Space)
  • Arrow keys — Step timeline (Shift for bigger steps)
  • Click a circle — View conflict details
  • Era presets — Quick jump to a period
